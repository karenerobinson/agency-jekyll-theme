Is there cell coverage or Wi-Fi?

Assume not. Cell coverage in the canyon and at Mack Dick Pavilion is little to non-existent. Expect state run Wi-Fi at the pavilion to almost, but infuriatingly not quite work. :(

Can I call a ride-share from the venue?

Assume not. Lyft and Uber are in the area, but spotty cell phone coverage and few drivers in the area will make this dicey. Plan ahead accordingly.
